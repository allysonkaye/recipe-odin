Kimchi Fried Rice

Ingredients

    Spicy Sauce

  • 3 tablespoon gochujang
  • 2 tablespoon maple syrup or granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic minced
  • ½ teaspoon gochugaru (Korean chili powder)

    • Main Ingredients

  • 10 ounces Korean rice cakes
  • 1 and ½ cup water
  • 5 ounces vegan cocktail sausages optional
  • 1 stalk green onions cut into 2-inch strips
  • one batch of vegan "fish" cakes optional, see notes
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds for topping

Instructions

  1. Prepare the spicy sauce: in a small bowl, stir together the sauce ingredients until fully combined.
  2. Transfer the sauce to a skillet or large saucepan. Add the rice cakes and cover with the water. Bring to a simmer and let simmer uncovered for about 8 minutes over medium heat.
  3. After 8 minutes, stir in the green onions, vegan cocktail sausages, and vegan "fish" cakes if using. Let simmer for another 7-8 minutes, or until rice cakes are very tender and the sauce has thickened. If the sauce is too thick, feel free to add more water.
  4. Divide between serving bowls and top with toasted sesame seeds!
  5. Tteokbokki will keep for up to 2 days in the refrigerator. Reheat over medium heat for 7-8 minutes, adding a few tablespoons of water if the sauce is too thick.
